Milestone 1

Purpose, Audience, Goal

Purpose: The purpose of this website will be a professional portfolio that highlights projects I’ve worked on, my technical proficiencies, and my approach to problem-solving.

Audience: The intended audience for my portfolio site is tech industry leaders like hiring managers, recruiters, and experienced developers, as well as peers and potential collaboration partners.

Goal: The main goal of my portfolio is to make it easy for potential employers or collaborators to contact me with opportunities. A secondary goal is to showcase my projects/experience and establish my personal brand.

Persona

Name: Jessie Wilder

Age: 30 years old

Profession: Founder and CEO of MindSoothe Inc., a startup specializing in digital solutions for mental health.

Company Details:

  • Name: MindSoothe Inc.
  • Focus: MindSoothe focuses on developing innovative digital tools and platforms to support mental health. They offer apps and web-based solutions that help users manage stress, anxiety, and promote overall mental well-being.
  • Stage: The startup is in its early growth phase, having successfully secured its Series A funding. They are now looking to expand their team and scale their product offerings.
  • Culture: MindSoothe has a culture that emphasizes empathy, innovation, and the well-being of both its users and employees. They value creative problem-solving, collaboration, and are committed to making a positive impact in the mental health space.

Technology Preference: Jessie is tech-savvy, often using the latest gadgets. She primarily works on a MacBook Pro and relies on her iPhone 14 for on-the-go management. She has a general grasp of software development (enough to be dangerous) but doesn’t consider herself a developer. Jessie values technology that is intuitive, user-friendly, and efficient.

Attitudes and Behavior: Jessie is passionate about mental health and technology's role in improving it. She's entrepreneurial, driven, and always looking for innovative solutions. Jessie values clear communication and quick decision-making. She spends a significant amount of time networking in tech and wellness circles and is always on the lookout for emerging trends and potential hires.

Frustrations and Needs:

  • Frustrations: Finding the right talent that not only has the technical skills but also aligns with the company’s mission and culture. Balancing the fast-paced growth of the startup with maintaining a focus on quality and user experience.
  • Needs: A full-stack developer who is versatile, adaptable, and has a genuine interest in mental health. Someone who can contribute to both the technical and creative aspects of their projects, and understands the importance of user-centered design.

Goals: Jessie wants to be able to quickly assess whether a candidate is a good technical and cultural fit. She is willing to spend a little more time on a portfolio if it demonstrates a clear alignment with her company's mission, shows creative problem-solving, and reflects a genuine passion for making a positive impact in the field.

User Story: As a user, I need to quickly discern if this candidate is suitable for our full-stack developer position and aligns with our cultural values.
